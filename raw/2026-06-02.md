- 출처: https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/01/this-ai-weather-startup-is-out-forecasting-government-agencies/
- 발행: 2026-06-01
- 본문:
  A new AI weather forecasting tool released by the startup WindBorne Systems offers more frequent and accurate predictions on key variables than the world-leading system developed by European governments.
  The release marks version six of their model, WeatherMesh, which the company says is more accurate than traditional and AI forecasts produced by the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF).
  WeatherMesh-6 produces a forecast every hour, as opposed to every six hours as traditional models do, with resolution down to 3 km in Europe and the continental U.S.
  According to WindBorne's chief product officer, WeatherMesh-6 "is as accurate five days out as a traditional forecast is the day before," particularly on surface temperature measurements.
  The model is powered by transformer-based AI models running on GPUs and produces forecasts in seconds—up to 100,000x faster and with a fraction of the compute cost compared to traditional numerical weather prediction.
