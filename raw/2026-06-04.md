- 발행: 2026-06-01
- 본문:
  WindBorne Systems released the sixth version of its AI forecasting model, WeatherMesh 6, on June 1, 2026; the company says WeatherMesh is more accurate than traditional and AI forecasts produced by the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF).
  WeatherMesh-6 produces a forecast every hour, as opposed to every six hours, as traditional models do, and its resolution is now down to 3 km in the continental U.S.
  WeatherMesh-6 "is as accurate five days out as a traditional forecast is the day before," particularly on surface temperature measurements.
  The company now has about 400 balloons in flight gathering sensor readings at any given time, launched from 15 sites around the globe.
  WindBorne, which was backed with $25 million in funding and valued at about $85 million in 2024, sells data to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration and the US military.
